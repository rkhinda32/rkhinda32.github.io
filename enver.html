    Academics that we offer are English, Math, Social Studies, Science, and Physical Education. We also offer honors courses. Advanced Placement or AP are offered in grade 11 and 12. The courses are English, Calculas (another word for math), Art, and any of the sciences. Second languages are also offered, which are French, Spanish, and Punjabi. For elective areas, we have 2 main areas of Applied Skills, which include Bussiness, Computers, Woodwork, Metalwork, Electronics, Engineering, and also Foods or Cook Training. The Fine Arts courses that we have are Dance, Art, Media, Band, and Photography.

    We also have International Students that come to our school and study for one or two semesters. The number of International students doubled form 2010-1011.

    Our school is involved in Community Services like Blood Donation and protecting the environment.
